Rain / Heat Backup Plan

These are also used by Rainy mode in the smart planner.

☔ Rain Plan

  • Boardwalk arcades
  • Music Pier or indoor show
  • Downtown shopping / Market-style browsing
  • Longer lunch or early dinner
  • Hotel pool if indoor/covered

🔥 Extreme Heat

  • Outdoor activities before 10:30 AM
  • AC break noon–3:30 PM
  • Hydrate hourly
  • Evening boardwalk after dinner
  • Keep Day 4 as a reset day

🍕 Pizza / Food Notes

  • Manco is kept as Google Maps only
  • Alternatives: Piccini, Mario's, Three Brothers, or nearest boardwalk food
  • Eat earlier with kids to avoid long waits
